Welcome to the inaugural ceremony of Asia-Pacific Alliance of Graduate Student Association (APAGSA), a non-profit organization, to facilitate the communication and cooperation among the graduate student associations in the Asia-Pacific region.

Asia-Pacific Region is acting an increasingly important role in the global political, economic and cultural contexts. Given this background, graduate students, as the backbone of the countries in the future, should broaden their horizon, expand the social network, and learn more about others and the world.

Graduate student associations have been well developed in many universities, aiming to enhance various aspects of postgraduate students’ campus life. However isolation between graduate student associations limits the graduate student associations’ ability to achieve the objective better.

We are united in our drive to build a dynamic and harmonious Asia-Pacific graduate student community by promoting and accelerating source integration, encouraging academic communication, enhancing welfare of postgraduate, and facilitating a favorable and sustainable cooperation platform
