Description

This month, I draw a dataset from National Bureau of Statistics of China. This report shows the result of the sixth census throughout the whole country, which was done in 2011. In addition, due to the limited access, I visualize the details using the data of the fifth census in 2000 instead. Meanwhile, to make some critical statistics more accurate, I also use the data from ChianToday.com.

Visualization library is amMap flash library.

The whole process spans from 1954 to 2011, which consists of 6 census in China. Since there are many administrative changes for some provinces, I have to made some trade-off with the only available reference map. The detailed data can be obtained from National Bureau of Statistics of China.

Result

I do not think I have conveyed the information effectively, but I at least I have learnt a lot from this trial.
